# English Reading Practice Exercises

Practice regularly with short, real-life passages and targeted questions to improve your reading skills, build confidence, and prepare effectively for the entrance exam.

---

## Exercise 1: Short Story

**Passage:**  
> Anna wakes up at 6 o’clock every morning. She brushes her teeth, eats breakfast, and then walks to school with her best friend, Minh. Anna loves reading books and playing badminton after class.

**Questions:**  
1. What time does Anna wake up?
2. How does Anna go to school?
3. What does Anna do after class?

---

## Exercise 2: Informational Text

**Passage:**  
> Lions are large animals. They live in Africa and Asia. Lions eat meat and usually hunt in groups called prides. Lions are known as “the king of the jungle.”

**Questions:**  
1. Where do lions live?
2. What do lions eat?
3. What are groups of lions called?

---

## Exercise 3: Email or Note

**Passage:**  
> Hi Hoa,  
> I am having a birthday party this Sunday at my house. The party will start at 5 pm. Please come and bring your favorite game!  
> Love, Mai

**Questions:**  
1. Who is the email from?
2. When is the birthday party?
3. What does Mai ask Hoa to bring?

---

## Exercise 4: Description

**Passage:**  
> My school is big and beautiful. There are many green trees and colorful flowers in the yard. I have many friends and friendly teachers. I love my school very much.

**Questions:**  
1. What is in the schoolyard?
2. How does the writer feel about the school?
3. Name one thing the writer likes about school.

---

## Exercise 5: True/False Questions

**Passage:**  
> Nam’s father is a doctor. Every morning, Nam and his sister have breakfast together. They go to school by bike.

**Questions:**  
1. Nam’s father is a teacher. (True/False)
2. Nam has a sister. (True/False)
3. Nam goes to school by bus. (True/False)

---

## Tips for Practice

- Read each passage carefully at least twice.
- Underline or highlight key words in the text and in the questions.
- Answer in short, simple sentences.
- Check your answers in the [answer_keys.mdx](./answer_keys.mdx) section after finishing.
